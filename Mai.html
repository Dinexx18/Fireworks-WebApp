About Us

Crafting Unforgettable Fireworks Displays

Founded in 2010, Mai Fireworks & SFX has become a leader in pyrotechnic entertainment. Our team of licensed professionals combines artistic vision with technical expertise to create breathtaking displays.

We specialize in choreographed fireworks shows synchronized to music, creating magical moments for weddings, corporate events, festivals, and private celebrations.

In addition to our event services, we're a trusted wholesale supplier of premium fireworks and pyrotechnic effects to industry professionals across the region.

Safety is our top priority. All our displays are designed and executed in strict compliance with local regulations and safety standards.
